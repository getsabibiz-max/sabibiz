Enter your purchase cost, transport, market fees, and how many units you bought. The Health Check is a free 2-minute quiz that scores your business out of 100. It asks 7 honest yes/no questions about how you track your sales, expenses, stock, debts, and profit. Your score tells you exactly what areas of your business need attention and what to fix first.
